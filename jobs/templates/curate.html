jobs.code4lib.org automatically pulls job postings from the code4lib discussion list. This means data entry of the job text, who the posting is from, and when it was posted can be done automatically using metadata in the email. We even automatically assign tags to postings using keywords from the job description.

But we need people like you to reliably evaluate these tags, assign employers, locations, etc. You might have a little bit of fun in the process since we use Freebase as a pool of entities to use in our descriptions.
